Sticky List lets you add customizable HTML slide panels to the left or right side of your website.

== Description ==



Sticky List is a versatile WordPress plugin that allows you to easily add custom HTML content as a sleek slide panel on either the left or right side of your website. Whether you want to display important announcements, a contact form, social media links, or any other custom content, Sticky List provides a user-friendly way to enhance your site‚Äôs functionality and user experience. With easy-to-use settings and responsive design, this plugin ensures that your slide panels look great on all devices, keeping your content accessible and engaging. Perfect for boosting interaction and providing essential information right at your visitors' fingertips.

== Why Use it? ==
- Render shortcode as panel content
- Add custom content (HTML or Text) as panel content.
- Anything you want as content.
- User friendly and easy to use.
- Customizable and responsive design.
- Easy to customize panel styles.

1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
